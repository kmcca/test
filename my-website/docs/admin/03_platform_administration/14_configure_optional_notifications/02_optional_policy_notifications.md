You can [subscribe to notifications](https://docs.veracode.com/r/t_configure_emails) for specific events that impact your policies to receive the emails listed in these tables.

<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Policy Configuration Change</span></p>

| Event          | Email Subject              | Sample Email Text                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | Recipients                       |
|----------------|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Policy changed | Policy Change Notification | Policy: {policy_name}. <br><br> Dear {user_name}, The policy assigned to your application(s) has changed. The policy currently assigned to your application(s) is listed above. Please visit the veracode platform for more details. | Users with access to application |
| Policy deleted | Policy Change Notification | New policies have been assigned to your application(s). Please visit the Veracode Platform for more details.                                                                                                                         | Users with access to application |

<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Upcoming Scan Required</span></p>

| Event                     | Email Subject         | Sample Email Text                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         | Recipients                       |
|---------------------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Scan due date approaching | Scan Due Notification | Visit: {link to application profile} for more details. <br><br> (Application Profile Page)Application: {application_name}, Policy: {policy_name},Scan Type(s): {scan types} Required By: {due date} <br><br> The policy assigned to your application requires you to complete a scan by the above date. Please visit the Veracode Platform to request the required scans. | Users with access to application |

<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Grace Period Expiring</span></p>

| Event                                     | Email Subject                              | Sample Email Text                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | Recipients                       |
|-------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Grace period for finding expiring         | Grace Period Expiration Notification       | Under the assigned policy, this application has at least one flaw for which the grace period will expire soon. You are required to fix the flaw(s) and resubmit your application by the date above. Please visit the Veracode Platform for more details.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          | Users with access to application |
| Grace period for score threshold expiring | Score Grace Period Expiration Notification | Application: {application_name}, Policy: {policy_name},Scan Type(s): {scan types} Required By: {due date} <br><br> Under the assigned policy, this application has failed the minimum score requirement. The grace period for bringing the application at or above the minimum required score will expire soon. You are required to fix some flaw(s) and resubmit your application by the date above to bring your application above the minimum required score. Please see the Detailed Report for more details. | Users with access to application |
