If an [Administrator requests that you change your password](https://docs.veracode.com/r/t_reset_password), you must reset the password for your Veracode account.

<p font-size="13pt"><b>Steps</b></p>

1.  In the email from Veracode notifying your of the reset request, click **Reset Password**.

    <p style="background-color:#FFFCF3; padding: 12px; border-left: 5px solid #F7CD55;">
    <b>Note:</b> The <b>Unlock Account</b> button is valid for one hour.</p>

2.  Enter the answer to your security question.
3.  Click **Reset Password**.
4.  Enter and repeat your new password.
5.  Click **Reset Password**.
6.  If your account requires multifactor authentication, follow the onscreen instructions for [Optional Multifactor Authentication](https://docs.veracode.com/r/using_password).
