"ft:prettyUrl" : "c_defining_course_availability"

You choose courses from a curriculum to make available to learners.

A curriculum contains one or more courses available to your learners. You can identify courses as:

-   Available to learners, but not required
-   Mandatory

If you have the Administrator [role](https://docs.veracode.com/r/c_role_permissions), you can:

-   [Create one or more curricula](https://docs.veracode.com/r/c_about_curriculum_dates)
-   [Assign a learner to one or more curricula](https://docs.veracode.com/r/t_assign_curriculum)
