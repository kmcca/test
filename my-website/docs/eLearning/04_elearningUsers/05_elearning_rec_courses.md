Veracode recommends eLearning courses to learners based on the results of their scans.

After a scan completes, the flaws are listed in the Triage Flaws page. In the details section of each flaw, Veracode provides a link to the recommended eLearning courses that teach more about the vulnerability and risk associated with each flaw.

Click the link to open the list of all the eLearning courses that Veracode recommends.
