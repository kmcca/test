You can change your passphrase at any time.

<p font-size="13pt"><b>Steps</b></p>

1.  In your IDE, select **Veracode** \> **Options** \> **Tools** \> **Veracode** \> **Credentials**.

2.  Click **Change Passphrase**.

3.  Enter and confirm your new passphrase.

4.  Click **Submit**.
